Our history

CS HR Partners is a young, dynamic company with 15 years of consulting experience. Our team has worked extensively across Europe and globally, consistently striving to deliver the highest quality service in every country. Today, we leverage our wealth of expertise to help you prepare for the future. ​

​ Our loyal customers, as well as those we will serve in the future, expect us to listen, understand, provide qualitative input from the job market and make strategic recommendations for their development projects. ​

​ Given our exposure to sensitive market and client information, we are committed to handling each mandate with the utmost confidentiality.

Meet our team

Charles SIGFRIT

Charles holds a bachelor's degree in Negotiation from KEDGE Bordeaux and a master's degree in Financial Management from EXCELIA. He began his career as an industrial management controller, contributing to financial missions and ERP implementations. In 2011, Charles joined the world of recruitment and headhunting, and had the opportunity to cover many international markets with offices based in Switzerland, France, the United Arab Emirates, Singapore, Indonesia and Canada. Today, he aims to provide his clients with advice that takes the best from each of these countries.

Charles partners with medium-sized and large companies, assisting them in the search, selection, and implementation of succession plans for Expert, Manager, and C-Level positions.

Charles is keen to help national and multinational companies organize their teams to generate growth. With a long-term vision and an eye for sustainable development, he will help you achieve and exceed your objectives.

Languages spoken: French, English, Spanish
Nationality: French

Senija CALDERONI

Senija, a graduate of EHL - Ecole Hôtelière de Lausanne, has been collaborating with Charles since 2022 in Geneva and Zurich. As a Talent Management Associate, she is currently building her own client portfolio while continuing to work alongside Charles on C-Level headhunting mandates.

Senija firmly believes that recruitment is the cornerstone of a company's success, making it crucial to match the right person with the right role.

Languages spoken: Italian, English, German, French
Nationality: Swiss

Christophe REYNAUD

Christophe has been working in the recruitment sector since 2019. His innovative approach has enabled him to achieve numerous successes, even in difficult times such as the Covid crisis. Christophe is an essential cog in the wheel when it comes to matching the best talent with complex business needs. After a career in business development in France and Canada, he has been recruiting IT profiles for companies in French-speaking Switzerland for the past two years.

Having lived in several countries gives Christophe the opportunity to offer his customers and talents the best of his different cultures.

His international background enables him to offer a unique multicultural approach, guaranteeing his customers tailor-made solutions and his candidates exceptional opportunities.

Languages spoken: French, English
Nationality: French, Canadian
